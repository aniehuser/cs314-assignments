Fun Pun Page!

If you love puns, here are some puns. If you don't like puns, here are some puns.

Lazy Kangaroo

Q. What do you call a lazy kangaroo?

A. A pouch potato!

Toothless Bear

Q. What do you call a bear with no teeth?

A. Gummybear

Space Party

Q. How do you organize a space party?

A. You planet!

Fancy Fish

Q. What do you call a classy fish?

A. Sofishticated

Melon Friends

Q. What did the watermelon say to the cantaloupe?

A. You're one in a melon!

Population Pun

Q. Which country's capital has the fastest growing population?

A. Ireland. Every day its Dublin!
